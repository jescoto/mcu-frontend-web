I am a young African-American entrepreneur from South Carolina. Because of trouble in the streets during my late teens, I missed out on both my highschool graduation and a guaranteed college scholarship. The last few years have been the toughest but I've found a way thru when others told me there was none. At present, I am working to glue all of the broken pieces back together in the face of seemingly insurmountable odds. And Im ok with that. Nothing worth having is ever suppose to come easy. I have no regrets. Only passion to take the lessons from my past and use them (pain & all) to take my share of success moving forward. I trade the forex and futures market for both income & investment. Im just open to learning as I go and networking with people who have been kicked down before but found themselves too darn stubborn to stay down when others thought they should. Cheers

John Smith
